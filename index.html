Who I am

Hi, I'm Christopher Ament.

For years, I built things and made art, searching for that elusive feeling of being fully realized. I found it in code.

There's an intoxicating energy in being around someone who is doing exactly what they're meant to do. For me, that moment of clarity arrived not in a studio, but at a keyboard. The logic, the creativity, the act of building from nothing—it was the missing piece. Nothing has ever felt more right.

Now, as a full-stack developer, I channel that certainty into crafting clean, purposeful web applications. When I'm not coding, I'm still creating—usually as a musician and audio engineer, or enjoying the beautiful chaos of being a husband and father.

My Approach

My approach to software development is centered around creating efficient, scalable, and user-friendly applications. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and following best practices to ensure the longevity and success of the projects I work on. Collaboration and communication are key components of my workflow. I enjoy working with cross-functional teams and believe that the best solutions come from diverse perspectives and open dialogue. Continuous learning and improvement are also important to me. I stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in the industry, and I am always looking for opportunities to expand my skill set and take on new challenges.

Projects

  1. Venu

    Venu - A real-time, location-based event discovery platform designed to drive community engagement. This full-stack application features secure QR ticketing with Stripe payment integration, significantly reducing fraud risk for event organizers. Implemented Firebase Cloud Messaging for targeted notifications, boosting event attendance rates. The platform enables users to discover local events, purchase tickets securely, and stay connected with their community through real-time updates and notifications.

  2. Pulse Health App

    Pulse Clinic - A comprehensive patient portal designed to streamline healthcare access. Built with React and TypeScript, this production-ready web application improves provider search, appointment booking, and medical record navigation. Collaborated with industry mentors and a 5+ developer team to ship core UI components including search functionality, modals, and theme guides. Prioritized accessibility-first design to enhance usability for patients with assistive technologies, ensuring inclusive healthcare access for all users.

  3. DevSync Project Manager Application

    DevSync - A real-time project management platform serving over 100 users. Built as a collaborative effort with 100Devs, this agile project management tool features Socket.IO integration that reduced task-update latency by 40%. Implemented Google OAuth authentication for enhanced security and integrated in-app chat functionality to strengthen team communication. The platform enables seamless collaboration and real-time updates for distributed development teams.

  4. Spectral Salon

    Spectral Salon - A modern, elegant website designed for a premium beauty salon in Agoura Hills. This responsive web application showcases the salon's sophisticated atmosphere and professional services with a clean, contemporary design. Features include service showcases, appointment booking capabilities, and a visually stunning gallery highlighting the salon's expertise in hair styling, coloring, and beauty treatments. The site creates an immersive digital experience that reflects the salon's commitment to luxury and client satisfaction.
