Who I am

Hi, I'm Christopher Ament, a full-stack software engineer living in Saint Augustine, FL — America's oldest city. Originally from Fort Lauderdale, I've always been drawn to creating and problem-solving, whether I'm working with code or mixing audio tracks. Outside of software, I'm a musician and an audio engineer. Music has always been a big part of my life, and when I'm not building apps, you'll find me playing guitar or working on sound projects. I'm also a proud husband and father, which means life is always filled with joy and a bit of chaos — in the best way possible. I love working on projects that challenge me and allow me to grow as a developer. If you're into tech, music, or just looking to collaborate on something cool, I'd love to connect!

My Approach

I’m committed to delivering projects that go beyond expectations, turning your ideas into reality. Here’s what you can expect when we collaborate:

  1. 1. Initial Consultation

    Every project starts with an in-depth conversation to fully understand your goals and vision.

  2. 2. Tailored Proposal

    I’ll provide a comprehensive project proposal, complete with visual mockups and a clear roadmap for execution.

  3. 3. Development & Updates

    Throughout the development process, you’ll receive regular progress updates to ensure everything is on track and aligned with your expectations.

  4. 4. Launch & Ongoing Support

    Once your project is live, I’ll be available for any support or adjustments needed, giving you confidence and peace of mind with the final product.
